How ProgressSDG Works

ProgressSDG is a tool to track completion of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) from the United Nations.

17 goals

150+ targets

200+ indicators

Launched in 2015, the 16 SDGs cover all kinds of environmental, social, and governance priorities. The 17th SDG measures partnerships required for completing the other 16 goals.

Each goal is made up of a set of targets, which are in turn comprised of numerous indicators. Progress data for targets was not available, so this project examines only goals and indicators.

The Summary section displays the goal number, title, progress as a percentage, and time remaining in years.

The Details section further explains the goal and its associated indicators.

The Assessment section examines the goal's trajectory and timeliness of its data.
